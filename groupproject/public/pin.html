Spring 2022 Professional Interaction Night

Save the date! At PIN, you can look forward to interacting with business professionals. It is a great networking event to gain insight on future internships and job opportunities.

DATE

Monday, May 6, 2022

LOCATION

Online TBA

AGENDA

5 pm to 6 pm HST

PIN is a semesterly networking event where professionals and students can have a discussion for around 30 to 45 minutes. Depending on the number of attendees, there will be a rotation of 2 to 3 business professionals per student.

Tips for Students

Make sure to present yourself professionally to employers

  • Dress in business or professional attire
  • Have your networking accounts, such as LinkedIn, up-to-date
  • Update your resume
  • Prepare a 15-30 second "elevator" pitch
  • Have a list of questions to ask your mentor
  • Bring a positive attitude!
