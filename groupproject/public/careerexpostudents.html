Spring 2022 Career Expo

The Shidler Career Services and Profesional Development Office will be hosting the Career Expo entirely online for Spring 2022!

DATE

Monday, February 21, 2022

LOCATION

Online TBA

AGENDA

10:30 am to 1:30 pm HST

The Career Expo is to host over 60 companies statewide in over 20 industries. It is designed to offer Shidler students a chance to meet with company representatives to learn more about what career options are available to them.

How to Prepare for the Event

Despite the event being held virtually, here's what you can do to present yourself to employers

  • Updated resume
  • Appropriate business attire
  • Research the companies you are interested beforehand
  • Prepare a 15-30 second "elevator" pitch
  • Have a list of questions to ask at the end of the meeting
  • Bring a positive attitude!
