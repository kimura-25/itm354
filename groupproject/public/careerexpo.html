Preparing for the Career Expo

The Shidler Career Services and Profesional Development Office will be hosting the Career Expo entirely online for Spring 2022!

DATE

Monday, February 21, 2022

LOCATION

Online TBA

AGENDA

10:30 am to 1:30 pm HST

Each company will be able to host 1:1 meetings with students and up to 5 group sessions throughout this 3 hour event. The 1:1 meetings will vary from 15 to 30 minutes long and companies can have a maximum of 30 students at a time. Your company can choose the times for your 1:1 meetings.

The exciting virtual event is a chance for Shidler students to meet with representatives they are interested to learn more about and career options are open to them. The career expo extends beyond academic instructional classrooms, and requires face-to-face interactions with professionals. This approach prepares students with the necessary skills for today's workforce.
