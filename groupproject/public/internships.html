  • The Shidler College of Business Internship Program provides both undergraduate and graduate students with opportunities to gain practical work experience. Students intern with sponsor companies for 150 hours per semester (15 hours per week).


  • The internship experience is counted as academic credit. Internships may be either paid or unpaid, as determined by the employer.


  • Once registered, a sponsor company can post their internships in our exclusive online database accessible to Shidler College of Business students. Companies can select from a talented pool of interns.
